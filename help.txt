Commands:
move [n,e,s,w] : Moves you in direction n,e,s,w
n,e,s,w also moves you in chosen direction
inv : Lists your current inventory
stat: Lists your current status
look: Describes your immediate environment
help: Displays available commands
die : Die, exiting the game
take [x]: Take [x], if there is one near you
use [x]: Use [x], if you have one
